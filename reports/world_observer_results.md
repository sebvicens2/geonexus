# EventGraph — World Observer results

> **What this measures (honest caveats).** This is a *descriptive* view of **media attention**, not a risk or forecasting model.
> - *Influence* ≈ how much an entity is covered (it correlates ~0.93 with raw degree); it is **not** real-world influence.
> - *Attention hotspots* blend connectivity + coverage + local density; they are **not** real-world risk.
> - *Causal paths* are **illustrative**: the event→asset links are hand-mapped heuristics, not inferred or predictive.
> - Sample = 2365 events over **14 days** (2026-05-20 → 2026-06-07) of English-language coverage (selection/coverage bias).
